# Privacy Policy — Recall by Helixion

**Last updated: April 15, 2026**

## Overview

Recall by Helixion ("the app") is a native macOS clipboard manager. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All clipboard history is stored locally on your device and never leaves your Mac.

## Data Storage

Clipboard history (text and images) is stored locally in your Mac's Application Support folder. This data is never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

## Third Parties

The app does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
